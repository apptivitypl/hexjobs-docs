## What is Hexjobs?

Hexjobs is a modern job search platform designed for the Central European market, focusing on Poland, Germany, Austria. We combine traditional job board functionality with cutting-edge AI technology to deliver:

- **Semantic Search** - Find jobs based on meaning, not just keywords
- **AI-Powered Matching** - Get recommendations based on your skills and experience
- **Multi-Region Support** - Search across multiple countries simultaneously
- **Real-time Updates** - Fresh job postings updated daily
- **Quality Analysis** - AI-evaluated job offers for quality and completeness
